Welcome

The Shell Piano Studio offers both private and group lessons to students of all ages and abilities in the Northwood, Iowa area.

It is my desire to not only teach my students to play the piano and to read music, but first and foremost to develop a lifelong love of music. After all, according to Johann Sebastian Bach,

“The aim and final end of all music should be none other than the glory of God and the refreshment of the soul.”

I want my soul refreshed forever, don’t you?

General Information

Private Piano Lessons are offered weekly in 30 or 45 minute sessions for students ages 5-105 of all abilities. Practicing is highly encouraged for students to gain the most out of our weekly lessons. Also students taking private lessons should already have or be willing in the near future to acquire a suitable instrument to learn on.

Students at intermediate or advanced level playing need more time than a 30 minute lesson allows. These students are encouraged to take weekly 45 minute lessons.

Buddy Lessons are available for siblings as well as friends who enjoy a more social approach to lessons. In buddy lessons we will take a full hour and have 20 minutes of playing together, 10 minutes of games together and 15 minutes one on one. This gives parents the opportunity to leave students for an hour.

Group Piano Lessons are offered for students who are looking to explore the keyboard in a fun group environment. These lessons are perfect for that person who wants to try it out, or perhaps just is looking for a low key alternative to traditional lessons.

Little Keys Group Lessons are offered weekly for children ages 3-6. This is a beginning music maker class. We’ll learn about the keyboard, and take our time learning concepts while having a ball!

Ignite Group Lessons are available for beginners of all ages, offered in 6 week camps during the summer.

Policy

Students are expected to arrive for lessons on time with all their materials and music. Parents and siblings may sit in the waiting area, wait in their vehicle in the driveway, or run errands so long as they return to pick up their student in a timely fashion.

Make up lessons are offered on a very limited basis for those who need to miss based on weather, illness, travel, etc. and only as my schedule allows.

Discontinuing lessons: Sometimes it just doesn’t work to keep taking lessons. Hopefully there is much thoughtfulness and discussion going into this. Please give a 30 day notice prior to discontinuing lessons.

Communication: best way to contact me is via email. Feel free to message me (Facebook) text, or I will take phone calls between the hours of 10 a.m. - 1 p.m.

Tuition/Fees: Tuition is due on the first lesson of the month. Payment not made by 10th of the month will result in a $15 late fee. I may be paid either with cash or check or Paypal (music2shells@gmail.com)
